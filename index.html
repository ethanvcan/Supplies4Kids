Supplies 4 Kids

By Ethan Can

Supplies4Kids (S4K) is a student-led non-profit organization that aims to donate and distribute school supplies such as pens, pencils, and notebooks, in addition to monetary donations, in order to provide educational help to disadvantaged children around the world.

Goal Statement

Our goal is to create a better learning environment for disadvantaged kids all over the world by providing them with school supplies through assistance from related organizations and individuals.

Vision Statement

In the future, Supplies4Kids plans to additionally provide books and workshops to children along with the donated school supplies to further their educational experience.
